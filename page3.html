the weekend

DESTINATION

Bethel, Maine

The small town of Bethel, Maine, just 1.5 hours north of Portland is located along the Androscoggin River and huddled at the base of Paradise Hill

We’re excited to spend a weekend with you in a place that is so special to us.

GETTING THERE

BY PLANE

Asheville’s (AVL) own airport is charming, but if you’re looking for more budget-friendly fares, try Charlotte (CLT) or Greenville (GSP). We suggest renting a car once you arrive so you have the chance to explore.

BY CAR

If you have time for a drive, there’s really nothing better than ending a road trip in the Blue Ridge Mountains. Asheville is conveniently located at the intersection of I-40 (East to West) and I-26 (North to South).
